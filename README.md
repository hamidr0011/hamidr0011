### Who I am

I'm Hamid. CS student, mostly self-directed. I build backend systems, mobile apps, and anything that involves making AI actually useful in the real world — not just demoed in a notebook.

Most of what I work on is pointed at problems I see in Pakistan, where good software is either missing or just hasn't arrived yet. I care more about something working for a real person than it looking good in a presentation.
